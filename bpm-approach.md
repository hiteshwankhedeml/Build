# BPM Approach

* It us to identify, analyze, measure and improve an organizations processes
* BPM is not to be seen as just another software, but rather as a strive for operational excellence
* BPM Lifecycle:
  * Design: Identify and author the process on paper
  * Model: Model the process on BPM system, like approvals based on invoice amount
  * Implement
  * Monitor
  * Optimize: As per feedback improve
  * Re-engineering: Add more automation
